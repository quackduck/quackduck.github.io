Shout About UAE

The purpose of this site is to inform you, surprise you, and help you with life in Dubai and the UAE. This site contains a vast range of information: from good restaurants and attractions (like the Burj Khalifa) to communities and transport options, our diverse range of content aims to cater to the needs of everyone. We will also help you find out about good schools, hospitals and extremely important info like emergency numbers of the Police or Ambulance

Dubai is a wonderful hub of technology, culture and architecture. The purpose of this website is to help you navigate and discover this city's many attractions, and make yourself comfortable here. Use this website as a guide to overcoming the many challenges you may face as an immigrant. It contains information about transport, laws, authorities and more, that will be vital to your everyday life here.
